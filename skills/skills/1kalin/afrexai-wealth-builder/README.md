# Wealth Builder — Personal Finance & Investment Engine

Your AI-powered personal wealth advisor. Build, protect, and grow wealth through structured financial planning, disciplined investing, and tax optimization.

## What This Does

Turns your AI agent into a comprehensive financial planning partner that covers:

- **Financial Health Assessment** — Net worth tracking with 0–100 health scoring
- **Cash Flow Mastery** — Budget frameworks that actually build wealth
- **Debt Destruction** — Avalanche, snowball, and hybrid payoff strategies
- **Investment Strategy** — Portfolio construction from 3-fund to factor-tilted
- **Tax Optimization** — Account priority, Roth vs. Traditional, tax loss harvesting
- **Real Estate Analysis** — Buy vs. rent calculator, investment property metrics
- **Alternative Assets** — Bitcoin position sizing, REITs, commodities allocation
- **Retirement Planning** — FIRE variants, withdrawal strategies, bucket approach
- **Estate Planning** — Will, trust, beneficiary, and asset protection checklists
- **Ongoing Monitoring** — Weekly/monthly/quarterly review templates
