# Agency Command Center

Complete operations system for service agencies, consultancies, and freelance businesses. From first inquiry to recurring revenue machine.

## What This Does

Turns your AI agent into a full agency operations manager:

- **Client Acquisition** — Lead scoring, discovery call frameworks, outreach templates
- **Pricing & Proposals** — 3-tier pricing calculator, value-based proposal structure, retainer design
- **Client Onboarding** — 48-hour checklist, welcome templates, expectations agreements
- **Project Delivery** — Milestone tracking, scope creep management, QC checklists
- **Team Management** — Utilization tracking, hiring decisions, delegation framework
- **Financial Management** — P&L dashboard, cash flow rules, late payment sequences
- **Client Retention** — Health scoring, QBR templates, expansion revenue playbook
- **Growth Strategy** — Capacity planning, revenue concentration alerts, productization ladder
